Privacy Policy

Reflexion

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reflexion ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using Reflexion, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Reflexion is designed to collect only the information needed to provide the App, improve the experience, and support optional purchases.

Information stored on your device

Reflexion stores gameplay progress and preferences on your device. This may include reaction test results, personal bests, session history, achievement progress, app settings, selected theme, and other gameplay-related data.

This information is used to show your history, calculate stats, unlock achievements, remember your settings, and provide the core app experience.

Usage and analytics data

Reflexion may use Firebase Analytics or similar analytics tools to understand how the App is used and to help improve the experience. Analytics may include information such as app opens, screen views, feature usage, session activity, device type, operating system version, and general location such as country or city level.

Reflexion does not require you to create an account to use the core experience, and analytics are used to understand general app performance and usage patterns rather than to personally identify you.

Crash and diagnostics data

Reflexion may collect crash reports or diagnostic information to help identify bugs, improve stability, and maintain app performance. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and related technical details.

In-App Purchases

Reflexion may offer an optional Pro upgrade or other in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Apple may provide limited purchase-related information needed to confirm purchases, restore purchases, and unlock paid features in the App.

Advertising

Reflexion does not currently require advertising to use the core experience. If advertising is added in a future version, ads may be served by third-party advertising partners, and this policy will be updated to describe the advertising data that may be collected or used.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Save your progress, results, preferences, and achievements
  • Show reaction history, trends, and performance insights
  • Process and restore optional purchases
  • Understand general app usage and improve features
  • Identify bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Protect the security and integrity of the App

3. Third-Party Services

Reflexion may use third-party services to support app functionality, analytics, diagnostics, and purchases.

  • Apple App Store and StoreKit for in-app purchases and purchase restoration
  • Firebase Analytics or similar analytics tools for app usage analytics
  • Crash reporting or diagnostics tools, if included in the App

4. Data Retention

Gameplay data stored locally remains on your device unless you delete the App, reset your device, or clear app data. Analytics and diagnostic data handled by third-party services are retained according to those providers' own policies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may delete the App to remove locally stored Reflexion data from your device
  • You may manage analytics and privacy-related settings through your device settings, where available
  • You may restore eligible purchases through Apple’s App Store systems
  • You may contact us with privacy questions or data-related requests

6. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Reflexion is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.
